Municipal Court sessions will now be conducted virtually/remotely to minimize exposure to COVID-19. Accordingly, all complainants, defendants, attorneys, victims are to contact the municipal court via email at jandrews@rutherfordboronj.com or at (201) 460-3030 to provide your full name, e-mail address, and cell phone number within seven days of a court notice.

The Municipal Court is responsible for processing criminal and traffic complaints issued within the town’s jurisdiction. Court sessions are held every Tuesday at 2:00 pm. Please call the office if you wish to plead not guilty.
